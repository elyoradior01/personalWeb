UNO Game in C#


   As part of our final project in the Application Programming course, we were tasked with creating an UNO game utilizing the C# programming language. We began by selecting a specific set of rules for the game (as UNO has multiple versions) and divided the project into various components such as designing the main menu, card layout, and different card functionalities such as reverse, skip, wild, plus two, plus four, etc. Additionally, we created the deck and incorporated background music while ensuring the game could be played by two players. Throughout the project, we collaborated effectively on GitHub, working efficiently over the given time frame of just over a month and a half. My specific responsibilities included coding the background music, player's hand cards, draw card, wild and plus four action cards, as well as contributing to the overall design.

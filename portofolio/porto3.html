Powtaytow Website and Social Media


   Our Statistics for Business class project required our team to assist a small snack business with rebranding and creating a website. We chose to work with Powtaytow, a micro, small, and medium-sized enterprise (MSME) in the snack industry, which can be found on Instagram under the handle @powtaytow.id. As part of the project, I was responsible for conceptualizing the new logo and brand notion, copywriting, and aiding in designing the website. Our team's collaboration and creativity proved successful, as the business owner expressed great satisfaction with their rebrand, including the new logo and brand image, as well as the design of their new website.

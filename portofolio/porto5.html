Ponose Coffee Cashier Aplication


   In this project, my team and I created a cashier application for our System Analysis and Design class. My role was mainly designing the application interface and also helped with creating the database.

   Ponose Coffee itself is a MSME (Micro, Small, Medium Enterprises) business engaged in the Coffee Shop field. Through deeper observations, we discovered that Ponose Coffee was having difficulties with their operational costs regarding their cashier application. In addition, they also wanted their system to have a range of other features such as loyalty programs, promotional offers, and detailed sales reporting.

   Through the utilization of our cashier application, Ponose was able to reduce their operational cost, as well as having better management over their inventory, stock, and sales.

   Ponose has been able to effectively reduce their operational expenses, while simultaneously gaining oversight and control over their inventory, stock, and sales.
